About Us

Design is an impactful strategic asset that is most effective when employed early in business branding. Grapixi designs purposeful and delightful experiences that captivate the curiosity and attention of viewers.

Grapixi is a multidisciplinary design studio that envelopes designing and branding, digital experiences and advertising, event management, and marketing. The digital world is forever changing and evolving. We try to keep pace with our inventive marketing strategies to reach out to a greater audience. Our company spearheads the idea of delivering our clients with pure innovations that reflects the passion and commitment we have for our craft and creativity.
